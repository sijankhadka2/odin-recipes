Lasagna

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, meat or vegetables,
and cheese, typically topped with a tomato-based sauce. The pasta sheets are usually
made from durum wheat semolina and eggs, and the dish is often baked in the oven
until the cheese is melted and bubbly. Variations of lasagna can include different
types of meat, such as ground beef or sausage, or vegetables like spinach or eggplant.
It is usually served as a main course and can be accompanied with a salad or bread.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Meat/Vegetables
  • Cheese
  • Tomato sauce



Momo

Momo is a popular dish in Nepal, it is a type of steamed dumpling filled with either
meat or vegetables and is served with a spicy dipping sauce. The dough for the wrapper is
typically made from flour and water, and the filling can vary depending on the recipe.
Momos are typically cooked by steaming, although they can also be fried or pan-seared.
They are a popular street food in Nepal and can also be found in restaurants.

Ingredients

  • Keema/Vegetables
  • Spicy Tomato Sauce
  • Flour



Fried Chicken

Fried chicken is a classic dish that is enjoyed around the world. It typically consists of chicken
pieces that have been coated in a seasoned flour mixture, then deep-fried until crispy and
golden brown. It can be enjoyed as a main dish or as a snack or appetizer.
it can be sweet, spicy, or savory depending on the marinade, coating, or seasoning used.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Pepper
